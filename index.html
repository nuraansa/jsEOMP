J E W E L S

All of our Jewellery is made with a Stainless Steel base and a 14k or 18k gold plating, making them completely Water and Tarnish Proof.

DURABLE:
Stainless steel is resistant to rust. It ranks 6 on the MOH (Mineral Hardness), while other metals such as silver ranks at 2.5. That means stainless steel is harder and can last longer than other metals such as silver and brass.
DOESN'T TARNISH:
Stainless steel doesn't tarnish because of an invisible layer of chromium that prevents oxidation from occurring. If you're tired of high-maintenance jewellery, stick with stainless steel as it is resistant to rust, discoloration and oxidization.
ENVIROMENTAL FRIENDLY:
Stainless Steel is 100% recyclable and is therefore a green product. Stainless Steel manufacturing saves non-renewable resources such as gold and silver. Compared to other options, stainless steel produces less waste during the manufacturing proces.
HYPOALLERGENIC:
Stainless Steel is also Hypoallergenic, allowing almost everyone to be able to wear our jewellery without irritation or infection.
